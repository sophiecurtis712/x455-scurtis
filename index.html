Welcome to The Perch! This website all about the different birds in San Francisco. We’re two friends who love noticing and learning about SF’s feathered locals. This website is here to help you explore birding spots around the city, meet the birds themselves, and pick up tips on how to bird. Whether you live here or are just visiting, there’s something here for every curious bird lover. We hope you enjoy exploring SF birds with us!
