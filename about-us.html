ABOUT US

Hi we are Lucy and Sophie! We’re two best friends from our hometown of Truckee, California, now living together in San Francisco. Our friendship has always been rooted in exploring the outdoors, and that carried with us from the mountains of Tahoe to the diverse habitats of the Bay Area.

Lucy is an avid birdwatcher who first introduced us to the world of birds — from spotting chickadees in the pines to identifying shorebirds along the coast. Through her, birding became something we could share, learn, and get excited about together.

This website is a blend of both of our passions. One of us brings the bird knowledge, and the other is an aspiring graphic designer who wanted to create a space that feels fun, approachable, and easy to use. We made The Perch as a place for us and our friends to share bird photos, learn together, and explore the city we love in a new way.

We hope you enjoy exploring SF birds with us!
