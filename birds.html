BIRDS

Our Bird Species pages celebrate the rich birdlife of San Francisco, from coastal shorebirds to park-dwelling songbirds and backyard regulars. Each profile includes quick identification tips, seasonal presence in the city, and the best local places to spot them — along with a curated playlist the bird might love, a poem that captures its personality, and videos that bring its behavior to life. It’s part field guide, part art project, and all about deepening your connection to the wild birds that share our city.
